The History Of The Melon

It all started in a small shop in Osaka, Japan. Our current and only, C.E.O Majima Goro was the founding father of the great Company, Majima Melons. Over the years, he has showed people the great and amazing watermelon dishes he, himself has created with massive brain and creativity. As more and more people were exposed to the greatnest of watermelons. the shop expanded and reached out into other parts of Japan.
